# Shopify Store

Manage your Shopify store from MOOD MNKY LABZ. View products, orders, customers, and more.

## Store Overview

The **Store** page shows:

- **Shop info** – Store name, domain, connection status
- **Stat cards** – Products, Orders, Customers, Collections, etc.
- **Quick links** – Navigate to each section

## Catalog

- **Products** – List and manage products
- **Collections** – Organize products
- **Inventory** – Track stock levels

## Sales

- **Orders** – View and manage orders
- **Customers** – Customer list
- **Discounts** – Discount codes and automatic discounts

## Growth

- **Marketing** – Campaigns and channels
- **Analytics** – Sales and traffic data

## Content

- **Content** – Pages, blog, navigation

## Finance

- **Finance** – Payouts, balances, transactions

## Prerequisites

- Shopify store connected via OAuth
- Appropriate Shopify API scopes for the features you use
