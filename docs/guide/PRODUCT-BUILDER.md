# Product Builder

Assemble a product from your fragrance oils and formulas, then push it to Shopify. The builder guides you through four steps.

## Step 1: Fragrance

1. Select a **fragrance oil** from your catalog
2. Search or browse by family
3. Click **Next** to continue

## Step 2: Formula

1. Select a **formula** – Candle, lotion, etc.
2. The formula determines wax type, wick type, and product structure
3. Click **Next**

## Step 3: Container

1. Select a **container** – Tin, jar, ceramic, etc.
2. Choose capacity (e.g., 8 oz, 4 oz)
3. Click **Next**

## Step 4: Review

1. **Product name** – Auto-suggested (e.g., "Lavender 8 oz Tin")
2. **Description** – Auto-filled from fragrance; edit as needed
3. **Images** – Add product images via Media Picker
4. Click **Push to Shopify** to create the product

## After Pushing

- Product is created in Shopify with the selected attributes
- Tags include family, product type, container, wick type
- Description includes fragrance notes and product details

## Tips

- Ensure Notion and Shopify are connected before building
- Add images in Step 4 for better product listings
- Edit the suggested name if needed
