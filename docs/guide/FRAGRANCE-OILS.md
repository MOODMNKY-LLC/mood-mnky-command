# Fragrance Oils

Browse and manage your fragrance oil catalog. Oils are synced from Notion and used across Blending Lab, Product Builder, and Studio.

## Browsing Oils

1. Go to **Fragrance Oils** in the sidebar
2. Use the **search** box to filter by name, description, or notes (top, middle, base)
3. Use the **family filter** tabs – All, Floral, Woody, Fresh, etc.
4. Click an oil card to view details

## Oil Detail Panel

When you select an oil:

- **Notes** – Top, middle, base notes
- **Family** – Fragrance family (e.g., Floral, Woody)
- **Description** – Synced from Notion
- **Image** – Thumbnail or assigned fragrance scene
- **Assign Image** – Link to Media Library or Studio to assign an image
- **Sync to Notion** – Push the current image URL to the Notion page

## URL Parameters

You can deep-link to an oil:

- `?oil=<id>` – Opens that oil in the detail panel

## Assigning Images

1. In the oil detail panel, click **Assign Image** or use **Studio** to generate
2. In **Media Library**, assign an asset to the fragrance (linked entity)
3. Use **Sync to Notion** to update the Notion page image property

## Syncing from Notion

Oils come from the Notion fragrance database. To refresh:

1. Go to **Notion Sync**
2. Click **Sync** for Fragrance Oils
3. New or updated oils appear in the catalog
