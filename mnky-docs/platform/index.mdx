# MOOD MNKY Platform & Services

The MOOD MNKY platform integrates various digital experiences and services to create a cohesive ecosystem for personalized self-care and wellness.

## Core Platform Components

### [Dojo Platform](/platform/dojo)

The Dojo Platform serves as a personalized "life OS" that provides tailored learning and development environments for MOOD MNKY members.

### [E-commerce Integration](/platform/e-commerce)

Our headless Shopify implementation powers the custom product creation and purchasing experience.

### [Personalization System](/platform/personalization)

Learn about our AI-driven personalization capabilities that tailor experiences to individual preferences.

### [Token Economy](/platform/token-economy)

Documentation on our engagement-based token system that rewards participation and enables premium experiences.
