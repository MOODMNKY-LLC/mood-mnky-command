## Overview

The Dojo is MOOD MNKY's premium membership platform designed to provide an immersive, personalized self-care experience. It combines our expertise in fragrance creation with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver a comprehensive wellness ecosystem for our members.

<Note>
  The name "Dojo" reflects our philosophy of continual growth and mastery in self-care practices. Just as a traditional dojo is a place of focused learning and development, our platform provides a space for members to develop their personal wellness journey.
</Note>

## Technical Architecture

The Dojo Platform is built on a modern, scalable architecture that leverages the latest technologies:

### Front-End

- **Next.js Framework**: For high-performance web application
- **Tailwind CSS**: For responsive, customizable styling
- **Vue.js Components**: For interactive UI elements
- **Progressive Web App**: Supporting mobile and desktop use

### Back-End

- **Node.js**: Server-side application logic
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication management
- **LLM Integration**: Connection to language model APIs
- **Docker Containers**: For service isolation and management

### AI Services

- **GPT Models**: For natural language understanding and generation
- **LangChain**: For AI workflow orchestration
- **Persistent Memory Store**: For long-term conversation context
- **Custom Training**: For MOOD MNKY-specific knowledge and capabilities

### Infrastructure

- **CloudFlare**: For content delivery and security
- **CloudFlare Tunnels**: For secure service access
- **Containerized Deployment**: For scalability and reliability
- **Multi-region Hosting**: For performance optimization
