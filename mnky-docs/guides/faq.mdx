# MOOD MNKY Frequently Asked Questions

This page answers common questions about MOOD MNKY's products, services, ecosystem, and company. If you don't find the answer you're looking for, please [contact our support team](mailto:support@moodmnky.com).

## General Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is MOOD MNKY?">
    MOOD MNKY is an integrated ecosystem of physical products, digital experiences, and AI-driven personalization designed to transform self-care from a routine task into a meaningful, personalized journey. Our ecosystem combines custom fragrance creation, digital self-care tools, AI-assisted learning environments, and a vibrant community platform.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What does the name MOOD MNKY mean?">
    The name "MOOD MNKY" reflects our playful approach to the serious business of emotional wellness. Like monkeys, we believe in curiosity, adaptability, and finding joy in the present moment. The name also represents our focus on emotional states ("moods") and how our products and experiences can help you navigate your emotional landscape.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is MOOD MNKY different from other wellness brands?">
    MOOD MNKY offers a unique value proposition through:
    
    1. **Extreme Personalization**: Truly bespoke experiences tailored to individual preferences and needs
    2. **Multi-sensory Integration**: Coordinated physical and digital experiences that engage all senses
    3. **AI-Augmented Wellness**: AI agents with specialized capabilities to enhance your self-care journey
    4. **Engaged Community**: Meaningful connections through shared experiences and collaborative creation
    5. **Accessible Premium Experience**: Making premium self-care more approachable through engagement-based alternatives to traditional payment
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Where is MOOD MNKY based?">
    MOOD MNKY was founded in Seattle, Washington, where we still maintain our headquarters and flagship store. We have grown to serve customers globally through our online platform and international retail partners.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Products & Services

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What products does MOOD MNKY offer?">
    Our product lines include:
    
    - **Custom Fragrances**: Personalized scent blends based on your preferences and emotional needs
    - **Candles**: Mood-enhancing candles with customizable scent profiles
    - **Bath & Body**: Self-care products designed for specific emotional states
    - **Home Fragrance**: Ambient scenting solutions with personalization options
    - **Digital Subscriptions**: Access to our Dojo platform for ongoing wellness support
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does custom fragrance creation work?">
    Our custom fragrance creation process works in several steps:
    
    1. Complete a scent preference questionnaire that captures your likes, dislikes, and emotional associations
    2. Our system generates an initial scent profile based on your responses
    3. You can refine this profile through further questions or with the assistance of our AI agents
    4. Once finalized, we create your custom fragrance formulation
    5. Your personalized product is crafted, packaged, and shipped to you
    
    The entire process typically takes 2-3 weeks from initial profile creation to delivery.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What is the Dojo Platform?">
    The Dojo is our premium membership platform that provides an immersive, personalized self-care experience. It combines our expertise in fragrance creation with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver a comprehensive wellness ecosystem.
    
    Key features include:
    - Personalized dashboard and content recommendations
    - AI-assisted guidance and support
    - Community features for connection and sharing
    - Digital tracking for your wellness journey
    - Rewards and recognition for engagement
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Are MOOD MNKY products sustainable?">
    Sustainability is a core value at MOOD MNKY. We implement sustainable practices through:
    
    - Ethically sourced ingredients from sustainable providers
    - Recyclable and biodegradable packaging
    - Carbon offset programs for shipping
    - Digital-first approach to reduce physical waste
    - Refill and return programs for product containers
    
    We continue to innovate in this area and are committed to reducing our environmental footprint.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## AI Agents

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What are MOOD MNKY Agents?">
    MOOD MNKY Agents are specialized AI assistants designed to enhance different aspects of your experience with our ecosystem. Our three primary agents are:
    
    1. **MOOD MNKY Agent**: Focused on customer experience and personalization
    2. **CODE MNKY Agent**: Provides development and infrastructure support
    3. **SAGE MNKY Agent**: Handles content, learning, and community support
    
    Each agent has distinct capabilities, knowledge bases, and interaction styles to provide comprehensive assistance.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do the agents use my data?">
    Our agents use your data solely to personalize your experience and improve our services. This includes:
    
    - Preference data to tailor recommendations
    - Interaction history to maintain conversation context
    - Usage patterns to optimize features
    
    We follow strict privacy practices, including:
    - Transparent data collection policies
    - Secure storage and processing
    - Options to control or delete your data
    - No selling of personal data to third parties
    
    For more details, please review our [Privacy Policy](https://www.moodmnky.com/privacy).
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I customize how the agents interact with me?">
    Yes, you can customize various aspects of your agent interactions:
    
    - Communication style preferences
    - Topic interests and expertise areas
    - Notification frequency and timing
    - Memory retention settings
    - Integration with your MOOD MNKY products
    
    These settings can be adjusted in your profile preferences on the Dojo platform.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account & Membership

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What membership options are available?">
    We offer several membership tiers:
    
    - **Explorer**: Free access to basic platform features and community
    - **Enthusiast**: Standard membership with full digital platform access
    - **Connoisseur**: Premium membership with enhanced features and benefits
    - **Curator**: Exclusive membership with personalized service and unique offerings
    
    Each tier provides increasing levels of personalization, access, and benefits.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How does the token economy work?">
    Our token economy rewards engagement across the MOOD MNKY ecosystem:
    
    - Earn tokens through platform participation, community contribution, and completing activities
    - Redeem tokens for product discounts, exclusive content, special features, and physical rewards
    - Progress through achievement levels to unlock additional benefits
    - Participate in limited-time events for special token opportunities
    
    The token system creates an alternative path to accessing premium experiences through engagement rather than traditional payment alone.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How do I cancel my membership?">
    You can cancel your membership at any time through your account settings on the Dojo platform or by contacting our support team. After cancellation:
    
    - You'll maintain access until the end of your current billing period
    - Any earned tokens will remain valid for 90 days
    - Your data will be retained according to our privacy policy unless you request deletion
    
    We also offer membership pausing options if you need a temporary break.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
