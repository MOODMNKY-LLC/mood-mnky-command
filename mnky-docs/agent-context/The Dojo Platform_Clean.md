# THE DOJO PLATFORM

## Overview

The Dojo is MOOD MNKY's premium membership platform designed to provide an immersive, personalized self-care experience. It combines our expertise in fragrance creation with cutting-edge AI technology to deliver a comprehensive wellness ecosystem for our members.

## Core Components

### 1. Member Infrastructure
- **Personalized Dashboard**: Custom user interface that adapts to individual preferences and usage patterns
- **Profile Management**: Comprehensive system for managing personal details, preferences, and subscription settings
- **Authentication System**: Secure, multi-factor authentication to protect member data and privacy

### 2. AI Integration
- **MOOD MNKY Master Agent**: Central AI assistant that coordinates all platform interactions
- **Customizable AI Personalities**: Members can personalize their AI assistants to match their preferences and needs
- **Memory Systems**: AI retains information about member preferences and past interactions to provide continuity
- **Natural Language Processing**: Advanced conversational capabilities for natural human-AI interaction

### 3. Content Delivery
- **Media Streaming**: High-quality audio, video, and e-book streaming services
- **Personalized Content Recommendations**: AI-driven suggestions based on member preferences and behavior
- **Interactive Tutorials**: Guided self-care routines and educational content
- **Exclusive Member Content**: Premium content only available to Dojo members

### 4. Scent Experience
- **Digital Scent Profiles**: Detailed tracking of member fragrance preferences and history
- **Custom Fragrance Creation**: Tools for members to design their own signature scents
- **Scent Memory Collection**: Archive of member-created fragrance experiences and stories
- **Scent-Triggered Content**: Media experiences designed to complement specific fragrances

### 5. Community Features
- **Member Forums**: Spaces for discussion and connection around shared interests
- **Direct Messaging**: Private communication between members
- **Event Calendar**: Schedule of virtual and in-person Dojo events
- **Collaborative Projects**: Tools for members to create and share experiences

### 6. Reward System
- **Engagement Tokens**: Digital currency earned through platform participation
- **Achievement Tracking**: Recognition system for member milestones and activities
- **Member Tiers**: Status levels that unlock additional benefits and features
- **Redeemable Rewards**: Ability to exchange tokens for products, services, or experiences

## Technical Architecture

### Front-End
- **Next.js Framework**: For high-performance web application
- **Tailwind CSS**: For responsive, customizable styling
- **Vue.js Components**: For interactive UI elements
- **Progressive Web App**: Supporting mobile and desktop use

### Back-End
- **Node.js**: Server-side application logic
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication management
- **LLM Integration**: Connection to language model APIs
- **Docker Containers**: For service isolation and management

### AI Services
- **GPT Models**: For natural language understanding and generation
- **LangChain**: For AI workflow orchestration
- **Persistent Memory Store**: For long-term conversation context
- **Custom Training**: For MOOD MNKY-specific knowledge and capabilities

### Infrastructure
- **CloudFlare**: For content delivery and security
- **CloudFlare Tunnels**: For secure service access
- **Containerized Deployment**: For scalability and reliability
- **Multi-region Hosting**: For performance optimization

## Member Journey

1. **Onboarding**: Introduction to platform features, preference collection, and initial personalization
2. **Discovery**: Exploration of content, community, and AI capabilities
3. **Customization**: Personalization of experience, AI assistants, and scent profiles
4. **Engagement**: Regular interaction with content, community, and creation tools
5. **Mastery**: Advanced platform usage, contribution to community, and custom experience creation
