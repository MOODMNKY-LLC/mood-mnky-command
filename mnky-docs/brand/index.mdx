## Brand Values

MOOD MNKY is founded on several core values:

- **Authentic Self-Expression**: Embracing individuality and personal stories
- **Holistic Well-being**: Nurturing mind, body, and spirit through multi-sensory experiences
- **Community Connection**: Creating meaningful relationships through shared experiences
- **Continuous Learning**: Supporting personal growth and development
- **Sustainability**: Prioritizing environmental and social responsibility

## Brand Voice

The MOOD MNKY brand voice is:

- Warm and empathetic
- Thoughtful and knowledgeable
- Creative and expressive
- Authentic and personal
- Slightly playful but never frivolous

For detailed guidelines on using our brand voice in communications, see our [Voice and Tone Guide](/brand/design/voice-tone).
