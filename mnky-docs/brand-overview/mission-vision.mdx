## How We Make Decisions

Our mission and vision provide clear criteria for prioritization and decision-making:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Mission Alignment">
    Does this initiative directly contribute to transforming self-care into a journey of personalized discovery and authentic expression?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vision Advancement">
    Does this move us closer to our vision of personalized wellbeing experiences that bridge physical and digital realms and foster genuine connection?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Value Expression">
    Does this embody our core values of authentic expression, curiosity, holistic integration, meaningful personalization, and accessible excellence?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Strategic Fit">
    Does this strengthen one or more of our strategic pillars without compromising the others?
  </Step>
  <Step title="Impact Assessment">
    Will this create meaningful impact for individuals, communities, or industries that we can measure?
  </Step>
</Steps>
