# MOOD MNKY Ecosystem Roadmap

<Info>
This roadmap outlines the strategic evolution of the MOOD MNKY ecosystem over a three-year horizon. It provides a high-level view of how our physical products, digital experiences, AI agents, and community initiatives will develop and integrate over time.
</Info>

## Roadmap Overview

Our ecosystem development follows a deliberate sequence designed to build strong foundations, create meaningful connections, and scale with integrity. This roadmap is guided by our [First Principles](/brand-overview/first-principles) and [Founder's Philosophy](/brand-overview/founders-principles).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Foundation Phase (Current - Q4 2023)">
    Establishing core infrastructure, initial product offerings, and platform architecture
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connection Phase (Q1 2024 - Q4 2024)">
    Deepening integration between physical and digital experiences, enhancing AI capabilities
  </Step>
  <Step title="Expansion Phase (Q1 2025 - Q4 2025)">
    Broadening product lines, community features, and expanding access models
  </Step>
  <Step title="Maturation Phase (Q1 2026+)">
    Refining the ecosystem based on data, evolving business models, and scaling impact
  </Step>
</Steps>
