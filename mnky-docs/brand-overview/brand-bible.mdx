### Writing Guidelines

<Accordion title="Vocabulary and Language Choices">
  <div className="text-sm space-y-4">
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Preferred Terms</h5>
      <p className="mb-2">Use these terms to maintain consistency and reinforce our brand identity:</p>
      <ul className="list-disc pl-5 space-y-1">
        <li><strong>Members</strong> (not customers or users) for those in our community</li>
        <li><strong>Experience</strong> (not product) when referring to what we create</li>
        <li><strong>Journey</strong> (not program or routine) for the process of personal development</li>
        <li><strong>Discover</strong> (not learn or study) for knowledge acquisition</li>
        <li><strong>Create</strong> or <strong>blend</strong> (not make or produce) for the act of making something new</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Sensory Language</h5>
      <p className="mb-2">Incorporate language that evokes the senses to create more immersive communication:</p>
      <ul className="list-disc pl-5">
        <li>Reference specific sensory qualities rather than generic descriptions</li>
        <li>Use synesthetic descriptions that cross sensory boundaries</li>
        <li>Include specific details that evoke sensory memory</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Sentence Structure</h5>
      <p className="mb-2">Vary sentence structure to create rhythm and maintain engagement:</p>
      <ul className="list-disc pl-5">
        <li>Mix short, impactful sentences with more flowing, descriptive ones</li>
        <li>Use active voice predominantly, with passive voice only for specific emphasis</li>
        <li>Address the reader directly to create conversation and connection</li>
      </ul>
    </div>
  </div>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Content Principles">
  <div className="text-sm space-y-4">
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Storytelling Focus</h5>
      <p>
        Structure content as meaningful narratives rather than collections of facts. Include specific details, emotional context, and a clear perspective. Every piece of content should have a beginning, middle, and end—even short-form communications.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Inclusive Perspective</h5>
      <p>
        Create content that acknowledges diverse experiences and avoids assumptions. Use inclusive language that welcomes people of all backgrounds. Review content to ensure it doesn't inadvertently exclude or stereotype any groups.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Emotional Intelligence</h5>
      <p>
        Recognize the emotional landscape around topics we address. Acknowledge challenges and difficulties alongside positive outcomes. Avoid toxic positivity that dismisses genuine struggles in favor of forced optimism.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <div>
      <h5 className="font-medium">Balance Aspiration and Authenticity</h5>
      <p>
        Create content that inspires without setting unrealistic expectations. Balance aspirational messaging with authentic acknowledgment of the complexities involved. Show the journey, including both progress and setbacks.
      </p>
    </div>
  </div>
</Accordion>
