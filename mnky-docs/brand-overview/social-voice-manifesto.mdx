# Social Voice Manifesto

<Note>
This is a starter document outlining the MOOD MNKY social voice framework. Future iterations will include more examples and channel-specific guidelines.
</Note>

## Our Voice Philosophy

The MOOD MNKY social voice represents the embodiment of our brand personality in digital conversations and community interactions. It acts as the consistent, recognizable presence that brings our brand to life in social spaces, reflecting our values while building meaningful connections with our audience.

## Brand Personality Traits

Our communication embodies specific personality traits that distinguish the MOOD MNKY brand:

### Primary Traits

- **Perceptive**: We demonstrate understanding of nuance and unspoken needs
- **Thoughtful**: We consider implications and show depth in our communications
- **Genuine**: We speak honestly without artificial hype or exaggeration
- **Warm**: We create a sense of comfort and welcome in our tone
- **Creative**: We express ideas in fresh, unexpected ways

### Avoiding

- **Pushy**: Never pressuring or creating false urgency
- **Flippant**: Never treating serious topics with insufficient respect
- **Clinical**: Never cold, detached or overly analytical
- **Exclusive**: Never creating in-group/out-group dynamics
- **Performative**: Never adopting trendy language inauthentically

## Language Guidelines

### Vocabulary Foundation

- **Sensory Language**: Rich, descriptive terms that evoke sensory experiences
- **Emotional Vocabulary**: Precise words for emotional states and experiences
- **Inclusive Terminology**: Words that welcome rather than exclude
- **Accessible Explanations**: Clear alternatives to technical or industry jargon

### Phrasing Patterns

- **Questions**: Opening conversations and encouraging reflection
- **Storytelling**: Narrative structures that create connection
- **Validation**: Acknowledging feelings and experiences
- **Personalization**: Tailoring messages to show understanding

- **Educational Value**: Making complex topics accessible
- **Creative Frameworks**: Innovative approaches to content

## Content Archetypes

We organize our social content into consistent archetypes that reinforce our brand voice:

### The Guide

Educational content that helps navigate self-care choices with expert insight delivered warmly.

**Example:** "When selecting a fragrance for stress relief, consider both personal preference and physiological effects. Lavender works well for many, but your unique associations matter most. Here's how to discover what works specifically for you..."

### The Confidant

Supportive content that validates experiences and offers understanding companionship.

**Example:** "Those moments when your self-care routine feels like another task on your to-do list? We've all been there. Sometimes self-care looks like giving yourself permission to simply rest. What does gentle self-care look like for you today?"

### The Curator

Thoughtfully selected recommendations that respect individual preferences while expanding horizons.

**Example:** "For those who found 'Morning Ritual' resonated with your senses, we've created three new blends that explore different facets of that experience. Each takes a unique direction while maintaining the grounding quality you appreciated."

### The Celebrator

Content that recognizes achievements, milestones, and the beauty in everyday moments.

**Example:** "Every self-care journey has its quiet victories. That moment you prioritized your wellbeing despite a packed schedule? That's worth celebrating. Share your recent self-care win, however small it might seem."
